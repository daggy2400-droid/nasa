RENDER POSTGRESQL SETUP GUIDE
=============================

1. Go to Render Dashboard: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click "New" â†’ "PostgreSQL"
3. Configure:
   - Name: melkoyai-db
   - Database: melkoyai_db
   - User: melkoyai_user
   - Region: Same as your web service
   - Plan: Free

4. After creation, you'll get:
   - Internal URL: postgresql://melkoyai_user:[PASSWORD]@[HOST]/melkoyai_db
   - External URL: postgresql://melkoyai_user:[PASSWORD]@[HOST].render.com/melkoyai_db

5. Use INTERNAL URL in your application.properties for better performance

6. Import schema:
   PGPASSWORD=[PASSWORD] psql -h [HOST] -U melkoyai_user melkoyai_db < supabase-schema.sql

This will give you a reliable database that's guaranteed to work with Render.
